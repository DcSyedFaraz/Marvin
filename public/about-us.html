About Us

4 coaches only is a company founded by former athletes and coaches.

What sets the 4CO team apart? Our enthusiasm for assisting student-athletes in the recruitment process is what drives us. 4CO loves talking ball and reaching out to college coaches. Nothing is more satisfying than submitting a non-discover student athlete’s information to a college coach and having the prospect excited about receiving a scholarship offer in a short period of time. Tons of student-athletes from division 1 to junior college were placed by 4CO.

WHY US? Our staff is made up of a diverse group of college-educated professionals with coaching and playing experience at the high school, collegiate, and professional levels.

WHO WE ARE!

Our team has worked with or as a high school or college recruiting coordinator. We also have on staff a former recruiting coordinator who knows exactly what college coaches look for during the recruitment process. We have placed over 500 student-athletes in college, from Division I to NJCAA. We understand what it’s like to do things the old-fashioned way, by pounding the pavement, sending films, and making many phone calls. We’ve also witnessed the satisfaction and smiles on the faces of student-athletes, parents, and coaches as a results of assisting their student-athletes in achieving their lifelong goals.
